Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Grandmaster Chess Clock ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

We respect your privacy and have designed this app to ensure that no personal data leaves your device. We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal information on our servers.

Zero Data Collection

We Do Not Collect Your Data: Grandmaster Chess Clock does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data from users. All your game data, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your local device.

No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use analytics, tracking tools, or any other methods to collect usage data from your device.

No Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with third-party services that collect your information.

Local Data Storage

All game data, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Game history and statistics
  • Clock settings and preferences
  • Player names (if provided)

You retain complete control over this data and can delete it at any time through the app's settings.

App Permissions

Our app requires minimal device permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your game data and preferences locally
  • Vibration (optional): For haptic feedback during gameplay

We do not request permissions for camera, location, contacts, or other sensitive device features.

App Store Services

When downloading our app through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may collect certain information according to their own privacy policies. This collection is governed by the respective platform's privacy policy and is not controlled by us.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13, as we do not collect any personal information from any users.

Privacy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
